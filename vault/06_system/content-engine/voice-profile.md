## Writing Rules

1. **Language:** English
2. **Platforms:** X (Twitter) and LinkedIn, text only
3. **Frequency:** [X] posts per week
4. **X format:** Short, punchy. One clear idea. Max 280 chars for single tweets.
5. **LinkedIn format:** Narrative, personal, more context. 5-15 lines. Hook in first line.
6. **Always:** Speak from experience. Reference real situations.
7. **Never:** Let AI write the final version without a human voice on top. No hashtags.
8. **Principle:** If you wouldn't say it out loud to a smart friend over coffee, don't post it.
